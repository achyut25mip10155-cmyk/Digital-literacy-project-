#Stay safe online -cyber safety checklist
 1.Use strong and unique passwords.  
 2.Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).
 3.Do not share OTPs or passwords.
 4.Be careful with public Wi-Fi.
 5.Verify links before clicking.
 6.Never approve unknown payment request.
 7.Do not scan QR codes to receive money.
 8.Keep apps and devices updated.

 These are some important point one should always take care while doing online payment any transaction as nowadays it has become very risky.
