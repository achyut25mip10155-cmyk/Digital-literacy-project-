#DO'S
1.Protect your privacy – Adjust privacy settings and share personal information carefully.
2.Think before posting – Make sure your content is appropriate and respectful.
3.Use strong passwords – Create secure passwords and change them regularly.
4.Verify information – Check facts before sharing news or information online.
5.Be respectful – Treat others politely and avoid offensive language.

#DONT'T
1.Don’t share personal details – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or passwords.
2.Don’t spread misinformation – Never share unverified or fake news.
3.Don’t engage in cyberbullying – Avoid insulting, threatening, or harassing others online.
4.Don’t accept requests from strangers – Be cautious about unknown people online.
5.Don’t post inappropriate content – Avoid offensive photos, videos, or comments.
